Hogwarts Houses

Known for its bravery, chivalry, and daring, Gryffindor values courage, determination, and the willingness to take risks to achieve one's goals. Gryffindor's are known for their bravery, as well as their strong sense of loyalty and friendship.

Ravenclaw is a Hogwarts house known for its emphasis on intelligence, wit, and creativity. Members of Ravenclaw value learning and knowledge above all else, and are known for their sharp minds and quick thinking.

Hufflepuff is a Hogwarts house known for its emphasis on loyalty, fairness, and hard work. Members of Hufflepuff value kindness and humility above all else, and are known for their dedication to their friends and their willingness to work hard to achieve their goals.

Slytherin is a Hogwarts house known for its emphasis on ambition, cunning, and resourcefulness. Members of Slytherin value self-preservation and the willingness to do whatever it takes to achieve their goals, and are known for their strategic thinking and ability to adapt to any situation.

  1. Quidditch

    Attention Hogwarts students! Tryouts for the Quidditch team are on September 30th at the pitch. Seekers, Chasers, Beaters, and Keepers needed. Brooms provided. Must be in good academic standing and have Head of House approval. Good luck! - Hogwarts Quidditch Coaching Staff

  2. Tri-Wizard Tournament

    Attention Hogwarts students! The Tri-Wizard Tournament is coming! Three schools will compete in magical challenges at Hogwarts on October 31st. To enter, you must be 17+ with Head of House approval. See Professor Dumbledore for more info on how to sign up. Join us for an unforgettable event! -Barty Crouch

    We announce the closure of the West Wing of Hogwarts due to a mysterious swamp that has appeared overnight. The exact cause of this swamp is currently unknown, and will be investigated. Until further notice, students and staff are advised to stay clear of the West Wing and refrain from attempting to enter the area. -Filch
